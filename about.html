About This Tool

This Real Estate Investment Calculator is designed to help investors analyze the financial viability of property investments.


Key Features

  • Cash Flow Analysis: Calculate monthly and cumulative cash flow.
  • Tax Benefits: Estimate tax savings based on German tax laws (AfA).
  • Optimization: Find the optimal rent or repayment rate for your goals.
  • Visualization: Interactive charts for debt, cash flow, and ROI.
